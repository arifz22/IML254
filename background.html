Background

"My background is rooted in a unique family dynamic, where I grew up surrounded by a significant number of female family members. Living with my late grandmother and aunties provided me with a foundation in understanding the basics of women's experiences and perspectives from an early age. I'm an ordinary boy who made the choice to move out from my family house, opting to live with my late grandma and aunties, mirroring the environment of my early childhood. While my life may not be filled with particularly extraordinary events, I find contentment in the simplicity of decent living. As an ambivert, I navigate life with a balance of social interactions and introspective moments, appreciating the ordinary yet meaningful aspects of my everyday existence."
